Assisted data management planning

Let us guide you through the process of scientific data management planning

You want to produce a lot of data?
-
Then you need a plan how to manage your data. You need standards, you need to assign roles, you need a description and you need a storage and backup plan.

We will speed up the process.
-
The DAMA International community has put a lot of effort in providing resources for professional data managers assisting them during the process and suggest some good practice. We use the terms and definitions from DAMA International in DMPcreator, resulting in a solid data management plan.
